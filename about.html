About Collaborate

Collaborate is a platform that bridges the gap between innovative startups and talented contributors like students, freelancers, and professionals. We empower early-stage ventures to find passionate contributors, while helping individuals gain real-world experience and build verified portfolios.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To create an accessible ecosystem where startups thrive through collaborative contributions, and individuals upskill through real projects.

Vision: A future where opportunity, learning, and innovation flow freely between startups and skilled talent from anywhere in the world.

Our Story

Founded by a group of tech enthusiasts, StartNet was born out of a college startup project that evolved into a purpose-driven venture. We realized the lack of scalable platforms where contributors could gain practical experience while helping real startups build products, and set out to solve that problem.

Meet the Team

We're a 5-member passionate team from diverse technical backgrounds, working together to deliver this product as our capstone project. Our team includes frontend and backend developers, UI designers, and project coordinators.

For Investors

If you're an investor or mentor interested in early-stage EdTech + JobTech platforms, feel free to connect with us. We're happy to share our pitch deck and traction plans upon request.
