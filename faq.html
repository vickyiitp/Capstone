How do I register on Collaborate?

Click on the Register button from the top navigation. Choose whether you're a startup or a contributor and fill in the details to create your account.

What kind of projects are listed?

Projects span tech, design, content, marketing, research, and more. Startups post real-world tasks, not just dummy assignments.

How does the contribution process work?

Once you find a project, click "Apply" to express interest. The startup will review your profile and skills before accepting you to contribute.

Are contributions paid?

Some contributions offer payments, while others offer certificates, experience, or mentorship. This is mentioned in each project detail.

Do I get certificates for my work?

Yes! Verified contributors can receive certificates from startups upon successful completion of tasks or milestones.

Is this platform free to use?

Yes, contributors can join and apply to projects for free. Startups can also post projects for free during the beta period.

Can I contribute to multiple projects?

Yes. You can apply to and work on multiple projects as long as you're able to manage your time and commitments.
